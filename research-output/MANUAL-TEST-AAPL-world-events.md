## Executive Summary

Apple faces a moderately supportive macro environment tempered by persistent geopolitical headwinds. The Federal Reserve's January 28 FOMC decision maintains current policy stance, providing economic stability while inflation remains monitored. Tech sector dynamics show exceptional semiconductor strength (+28% over 4 months), benefiting Apple's strategic Intel chip partnership exploration.

**Macro Environment:** The Fed's measured approach supports equity valuations, with Apple's 32.89 P/E ratio reflecting sustained investor confidence. However, elevated interest rates continue pressuring growth multiples across technology. Current economic data suggests a soft-landing trajectory, favorable for consumer discretionary demand.

**Geopolitical Risks:** US-China tensions remain the primary concern for Apple, given significant manufacturing exposure and China representing a critical consumer market. Tariff uncertainties and technology transfer restrictions pose supply chain vulnerabilities.
